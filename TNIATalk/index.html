Small Data/Slow Data (Not Big Data)

  • Data fits on a laptop
    • atleast laptop + usb drive
  • Small enough for human evaluation
    • Sampling rate is slow
    • Clinical trials
      • Duration: Years
      • Sampling rate: Weeks/Months
    • R&D Images (from new types of devices)
      • Duration: Month(s) to Year(s)
      • Sampling rate: Weeks

Overfitting

  • Typically have months to "experiment"
  • Try everything -> something will work by random chance
  • Train, validate, test
    1. Train model on one partition
    2. Validate and tune on second partition
    3. Test on third (unseen) partition
  • "Testing" partition only valid once
    • As soon as you see it, it becomes a "validation" partition

Reproducibility Crisis

  • Challenges in Irreproducible Research
  • John Arnold vs. Bad Science (Wired)
    1. When people have lot of time to experiment with data...
    2. They find things that work by Random Chance
  • A particular concern with small data
